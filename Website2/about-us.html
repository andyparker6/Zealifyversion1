Meet the Team behind Zealify

The Team

The Zealify team consists of recent graduates that have experience of working in SMEs and an understanding of the recruitment process and the pains experienced by both employers and job seekers in the graduate job space. The team is able to see the situation from both sides of the fence having embarked on a job search during their year of university before opting to take an entrepreneurial route.

The Zealify team have set about re-inventing the traditional recruitment process in the aim of improving the situation for all sides.
